Our Services
 
You can choose across various materials form our false ceiling according to your taste, requirements and budget.
Plaster Of Paris
Plaster of paris is the most popular choice for false ceiling. It can be crafted in stunning patterns with without wooden panels and light textures.
Gypsum Board
Gypsum board is another popular choice for ceiling and offers a host of elegant designs. They are light weighted, could sustain ducting and easy to handle if you wish to play with the weight of room.
Thermacol
Fitted with aluminium frames and it is one of the most economical false ceiling.
PVC
Made from the robust plastic. PVC is a low maintenance for ceiling variety. It stays for years and it is available in wide variety of designs and colours.
T - Section
Our products are carved out of superior quality raw materials sourced from reliable vendors of the market. This product line caters approximately all needs and requirement related to this domain. We can see this kind of work in bank and office.
Wood
Wooden false ceiling have excellent natural patterns and textures that can please the eye. Wood is a good material to work with wooden panels can come in the form of hollow blocks, boards or panels.
